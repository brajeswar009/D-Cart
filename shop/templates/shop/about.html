D-Cart

D-Cart is an Indian e-commerce company based in Odisha, Telengana, India. It was founded by Brajeswar Lenka in the year 2019. The company initially focused on book sales, before expanding into other product categories such as consumer electronics, fashion, home essentials & groceries, and lifestyle products.The service competes primarily with Amazon's Indian subsidiary, and the domestic rival Snapdeal. As of March 2020, D-Cart held a 9.5% market share of India's e-commerce industry. D-Cart is significantly dominant in the sale of electronics gadgets especially Smartphones, smartwatches and also fashion accessories such as t-shirts and was described as being "neck and neck" with Amazon in the sale of electronics and mobile phones.D-Cart also owns PhonePe, a mobile payments service based on the Unified Payments Interface (UPI). Till 2022 it has plan to extend it's services to different countries and open a BPO in U.S. Many big companies wants to buy stocks of D-Cart because of it's increasing popularity among youths.
